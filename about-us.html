About Us

  TPBS Corp was established to provide efficient and expert accounting, taxation, and business consulting services to individuals and small businesses. Our services include tax preparation, accounting, bookkeeping, payroll, and other business-related services.

    Our goal is to make your tax preparation and accounting process easy, accurate, and efficient, in order to reduce your tax liability and thereby save you money. Our expertise in tax issues and tax laws will help our clients forecast their tax liabilities and help them achieve financial success.

    Inaissa Quinones, our founder, is an Enrolled Agent who has been diligently serving as a financial bookkeeper and accountant for more than 20 years. She has the experience and knowledge to provide her clients with excellent customer service, as well as encourage them with the correct tools to build an ongoing successful business.
